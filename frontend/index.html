Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Adelphos Tech.

How long does it take to build a mobile app?

A typical MVP mobile app takes 6–12 weeks depending on complexity. We begin with a discovery phase to scope the project accurately and share a timeline before development begins.

Do you offer fixed-price or time & material contracts?

Both. For well-defined projects we offer fixed-price contracts. For evolving products, time & material gives you flexibility to iterate as you learn. We'll recommend the right model for your situation.

Can I hire a single developer or do I need a full team?

Absolutely — you can hire a single specialist part-time or full-time, or scale up to a complete dedicated team. Our engagement models are designed to flex with your needs and budget.

What industries do you specialize in?

We've delivered solutions for fintech, healthcare, logistics, e-commerce, education, and real estate. Our technology-first approach means we adapt quickly to any domain.

How do you handle post-launch support and maintenance?

We offer ongoing maintenance plans that cover bug fixes, security patches, performance monitoring, and feature enhancements. You'll always have a dedicated contact for support requests.
